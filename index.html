Become a Member of Our Cinematic Community

Welcome to the world of films, stories, and discussions! We're thrilled to introduce you to our brand new Movie Club at IACS, a society of true cinephiles who are passionate about the art of filmmaking.

Our Vision: Through this initiative, we're aiming to celebrate our shared love for cinema and provide a platform for the IACS community to engage in meaningful film-related activities. Whether you're a dedicated film enthusiast or just starting your cinematic journey, there's a place for you in our society.

Membership Tiers:

Tier 1: The Thinkers: Join us for thought-provoking discussions about the art of filmmaking. We meet weekly to exchange ideas, analyze movies, and collaboratively select films for our screenings. Expand your knowledge and dive deep into the world of cinema with fellow thinkers.

Tier 2: The Viewers: If you're eager to enjoy exceptional films and influence our movie selection, this tier is perfect for you. Attend our monthly movie screenings and cast your vote for the featured film from a curated list. The most-voted movie will be screened for the month.

We're open to movie suggestions from both tiers, so your voice matters!
